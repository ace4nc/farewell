Class of 2026 Volume I — Issue I August 9, 2026

Rumour has it that the Class of 2026 may finally be getting the farewell they've spent months pretending not to care about. Naturally, nobody is confirming anything.

The details remain suspiciously scarce. Several students have suddenly become very interested in conversations that don't concern them, and a remarkable number of people seem convinced they know exactly what's happening despite having absolutely no evidence to support their claims.

As usual, the rumours are doing what rumours do best: spreading faster than facts.

The people who know what's happening aren't talking.

The people talking don't know what's happening.

From what we've heard, preparations are already underway behind closed doors. The people involved have remained frustratingly secretive, which, if history has taught us anything, usually means something interesting is worth keeping quiet.

Not that anyone is complaining.

After all, half the excitement comes from trying to figure it out. The other half comes from pretending you already have it figured out.

For now, all that's certain is that something is coming. And judging by the amount of speculation it's already generating, it might just be worth the wait.
